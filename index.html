Moduel rules

Game pieces There are three different game pieces, the Fan, the Core and the Turbine. These pieces come in different colors: blue, yellow, and red. The blue and yellow pieces represent the working parts, while the red pieces represent the broken parts that can only be used in repair stations. Robots can only carry one piece at a time, it can either be their teams color or red. If a piece rolls off the playing field, it is unusable for the rest of the game.
